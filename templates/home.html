News Article Summarizer


Reading the entire news Articles is sometimes cumbersome & time taking in busy lives.
This is a small attempt to collect the news articles on BBC.

Apart from original articles, this webapp provides the short summary of the news articles using NLP Techniques.
The articles are grouped into different categories based on their tags on original website.
